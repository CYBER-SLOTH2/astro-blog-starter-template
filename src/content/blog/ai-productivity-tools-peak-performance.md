Data privacy is a valid concern with any digital tool. ### Q8. What is the difference between AI automation and AI assistance?
**AI automation** involves tasks being performed completely by AI without human intervention (e.g., automatically sorting emails). **AI assistance**, on the other hand, involves AI working alongside a human to augment their capabilities (e.g., an **AI writing assistant** suggesting improvements to your draft). Both contribute to **AI driven efficiency** but differ in their level of human involvement.
