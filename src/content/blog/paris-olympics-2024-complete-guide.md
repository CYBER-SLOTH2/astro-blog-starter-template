The most revolutionary element of the 2024 Games is the decision to move the **Paris 2024 opening ceremony** out of a stadium and onto the Seine River.

## Looking Beyond 2024: The History and Future of the Games

While our focus is rightfully on the **Paris Olympics 2024**, understanding the context of the Games requires a look back at the **history of the Olympic games** and forward to their trajectory.

### Historical Context: Paris 1924 to Paris 2024

Hosting the Games exactly one century after their last turn in 1924 is a monumental nod to the Games' heritage. The 1924 Paris Games were significant for being the first to feature an Olympic Village and for debuting the official Olympic motto: *Citius, Altius, Fortius* (Faster, Higher, Stronger). The 2024 Games carry that same innovative spirit, particularly in venue planning and the inclusion of urban sports.

### The Legacy and Impact of the Host City

The **impact of olympics on host city** is always a mixed bag, but Paris has made sustainability and urban development central to its planning. By using 95% existing or temporary venues, the city minimized construction waste and maximized long-term utility (like the Olympic Village conversion). This focus sets a new standard for future Olympic hosts.
